 HaitiSafety

![HaitiSafety Logo](public/logo.png)

 Real-time safety, travel, and community intelligence platform for Haiti.

HaitiSafety is building the most trusted platform for travelers, residents, businesses, humanitarian organizations, and government agencies seeking accurate, real-time information about Haiti.

Our mission is to improve transparency through verified community reports, AI-powered intelligence, business reviews, travel insights, and live safety information.

---

 Mission

Provide trusted, real-time information that helps people make informed decisions while traveling, living, investing, or operating in Haiti.
